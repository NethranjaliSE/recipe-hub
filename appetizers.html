Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Mix chopped tomato, garlic, basil, olive oil.
  3. Top bread with mixture and serve.
